Who are we

We are a group of 2 University Students who have a passion for cooking and want to make a change to the rampant food wastage around the world. We leverage on advanced technology to make cooking accessible, enjoyable, and environmentally friendly. Our app intelligently suggests recipes based on the ingredients you already have, helping you reduce food waste and discover new dishes effortlessly.

We are committed to:

Enhancing Nutritional Awareness:

Providing detailed nutritional information for each recipe, helping you make informed dietary choices that fit your health goals.

Reducing Food Waste:

Encouraging the use of available ingredients to minimize waste and promote sustainability.

Fostering Culinary Creativity:

Inspiring users to experiment with diverse ingredients and cuisines, expanding their culinary horizons.

Building Community:

Creating a platform where users can share their culinary experiences and tips, fostering a community of supportive, like-minded individuals.

Simplifying Meal Planning:

Streamlining the meal planning process with tools for organizing weekly meals and generating shopping lists, making healthy eating convenient for everyone.
